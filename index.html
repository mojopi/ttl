TTL: Youth-Driven Community + Ed + Media Model

Wellbeing embedded in our assumptions + biases

Our "Biases"/Assumptions

  • Youth as producers, not consumers
  • Youth as leaders, not followers
  • Creative Expression > Information Transmission
  • Connection Across Difference > Silos
  • Human Scale > Social Network Scale
  • Youth Made Media Fostering Connection & Substance > Division & Aesthetics

Problem

Unprecedented youth mental health crisis & loneliness epidemic.

79% of Gen Zers identify as lonely.

According to U.S. Surgeon General: for health, loneliness is comparable to smoking daily.

Mission

Through our youth programming and media, we help adolescents throughout the world develop authentic connections, creative empowerment, a sense of purpose, & greater emotional wellbeing.

Vision

For every adolescent to feel seen, heard, and able to transform their experiences into media that helps themselves and others become more resilient in the face of their struggles.
